About

I am a UX designer/Web developer with a background in arts and education. As an artist and educator, I excel in collaboration, adaptability, and emotional intelligence. Specifically, as a UX designer, I am skilled in various types of research and prototyping. By researching, I can gain in-depth information about my user that I can implement when quickly creating many ideas in the ideation process. For me, this is one of the most gratifying parts. This process results in a highly functional prototype that can then be tested and further improved. I enjoy finding the best possible way to communicate my designs. For example, I can create design systems that exhibit the astounding amount of final design decisions I've made. My ambition is to design experiences that will put the user at the center to improve their lives in a meaningful way.
